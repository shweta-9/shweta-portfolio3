RISE

BOYS & GIRLS CLUB OF ALBION

2019

Rexpressions / Inspiring / Student / Empowerment

RISE is a youth empowerment program that aims to expose youth to the tools and resources they need to level up to their higher potential. This event intends to address gaps in knowledge regarding mental, physical, emotional, financial and technical skills. Each month, a different topic is selected and explored through interactive workshops, panels, guest speakers and more. The pillars of self-love are taught by exploring concepts of self-acceptance, self-responsibility, self-assertiveness, purposefulness, and integrity.

Energize. Uplift. Empower. RISE.
